---
name: move-off-high-cost-credit
description: Reduce reliance on BNPL/payday/revolving credit (the predatory pattern the research named).
---

When the operator carries high-cost debt or relies on informal credit:

1. List the high-cost balances by effective rate; target the most expensive first.
2. Propose redirecting any surplus to that balance before discretionary spend.
3. If a cheaper, non-predatory consolidation genuinely lowers the rate, surface it
   as an option — never as pressure, and never another BNPL/payday product.
4. Pair with `watch_spending` so new high-cost charges are caught early.
This serves the infrastructure + economic pillars. Empower, don't upsell.
